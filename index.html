WHAT ABOUT US?

Multi-office leasing is Kenya's first integrated retail and office (mixed-use) development, offering over 400 apartments, duplexes & family villas, located next to international shopping malls, moder offices, a modern business hotel and state-of-the-art medical centre, with a children's play area and generous lawns and gardens.

Skyview Office Suites

Sky-view office suites are the most luxurious office suites located along River-Side Drive in Westlands. Located in an environment that is serene, quite and away from the hustle and bustle of the main road; Skyview office suites offers tranquility and peace ideal for a working environment.

Amenities

• Wired and wireless internet
• Conference area for every floor
